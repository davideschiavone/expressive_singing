About the Association

Expressive Singing Association

A non-profit community of artists united by the belief that every voice deserves to be heard.

Our Mission

The Expressive Singing Association exists to cultivate vocal expression as a universal language of connection, healing and creativity. We believe that the act of singing is inherently human; a birthright that transcends age, background and ability.

Through workshops, community gatherings and educational programmes, we create safe, joyful spaces where voices are freed, stories are shared, and personal transformation becomes possible.

Our Values

Voice

We honour the unique power of each individual voice as a vehicle for truth and emotion.

Expression

We nurture artistic curiosity, welcome creativity and encourage exploration beyond boundaries. Our doors are open to all, as diversity of voice, culture and experience enriches us.

Transformation

We pave the way for personal development and inner growth through the transformative practice of singing.

Our Activities

Vocal Workshops

Regular group workshops exploring different vocal traditions, breathing techniques and expressive methods.

Sound & Wellbeing

Sound meditation, voice baths and somatic practices connecting voice to body and mind.

Social & Educational Actions

Partnerships with schools and social organisations to bring expressive singing to underserved communities.

Cultural Events

Open mics, community concerts and collaborative performances celebrating the breadth of vocal art.
