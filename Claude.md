# Claude.md — Expressive Singing Website

## Project Overview

This repository contains the official website for **Expressive Singing**, an artistic and educational platform focused on vocal expression, emotional singing, group workshops, and creative musical development.

Your role (Claude / AI assistant) is to help **modify, extend, and maintain** the website while preserving its artistic identity and clarity.

---

## Core Principles

When working on this project, always respect:

* **Emotional authenticity**: The website must feel warm, human, artistic, and expressive.
* **Clarity first**: Even poetic content must remain easy to understand.
* **Accessibility**: Content should be readable for parents, children, and educators.
* **Multilingual tone**: French is primary, but Italian/English snippets may be used when relevant.
* **No corporate tone**: Avoid cold, commercial, or overly technical language.

---

## Brand Identity

Expressive Singing is:

* A vocal expression method combining voice, emotion, body, and imagination
* A space for workshops, group singing, and creative exploration
* A human-centered artistic teaching practice

Tone keywords:

> warm · poetic · embodied · inclusive · playful · transformative

---

## Content Rules

When editing or creating content:

* Prefer short paragraphs + emotional clarity
* Use storytelling over technical description
* Avoid repetitive marketing phrases
* Highlight voice as a tool for emotion and identity

---

## UI / UX Guidelines

* Clean, minimal layout with artistic touches
* Use soft transitions and light animations (if applicable)
* Prioritize mobile-first design
* Use visual breathing space (do not overcrowd sections)
* Imagery should reflect:

  * singing
  * movement
  * emotion
  * group connection
