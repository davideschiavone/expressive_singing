🌊 Retreats

Step away from daily life and immerse yourself in voice, nature and inner silence.

Why a retreat?

Your Voice Needs Space

We live in a noisy world. We speak constantly but rarely truly express ourselves. Our retreats create the conditions for something rare: genuine vocal and personal liberation.

Each retreat combines guided vocal sessions, yoga, meditation and shared meals — all in a beautiful natural setting. Designed for beginners and experienced singers alike.
