The method

Grounded in Vocology

At Expressive Singing, free expression rests on solid technical foundations. Every class is built on Vocology — the science of voice habilitation and training — so that you sing not only with emotion, but with healthy, efficient and sustainable technique.

Your teacher is Imane El Baladi, Artistic Director of the association and holder of a Master's degree in Artistic Vocology. She combines rigorous vocal science — breath management, registration, resonance and vocal health — with an embodied, expressive approach, so that technique and emotion grow together.

The technical foundations

What You'll Train

Breath & Support

Efficient breath management as the engine of a free, tension-free sound.

Registration & Range

Smooth navigation between vocal registers to extend your range without strain.

Resonance & Placement

Shaping resonance for clarity, projection and your own unmistakable timbre.

Vocal Health & Longevity

Science-based habits that keep your voice healthy for years of singing.
