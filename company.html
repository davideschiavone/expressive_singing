Our Story

An Artistic Vision

Born from a profound belief in the voice as an instrument of transformation, Expressive Singing Company brings together artists who refuse to separate technique from soul. We create performances that speak to the human condition.

Our work spans contemporary music, interdisciplinary performance and educational concerts. We are based in Geneva and perform across Switzerland and internationally.

Current production

Soundtrack – Finding Home

What does it take to feel at home?

Soundtrack – Finding Home is a musical and theatrical performance that follows several characters arriving in Geneva, their suitcases heavy with memories, expectations and illusions. From departure to arrival, from administrative shock to solitude, each one crosses a city that first ignores them, then organises them, then puts them to the test. On stage, bodies, voices and songs tell of this passage — the struggle to find one's place, to belong, to recognise oneself in a new environment.

Through a succession of scenes weaving together monologues, live music and collective movement, the city becomes a character in its own right — cold, structured, at times impersonal, yet also alive with possibility. Little by little, the individual paths cross, the voices answer one another, and another form of belonging emerges. A tender, contemporary journey through exile, identity and connection, where stage and audience finally become one.

Duration: 90 min · All ages · French/English · Geneva premiere 2026

The artists

Who We Are

Expressive Singing is not a list of names — it is a family of voices. We come from different worlds: teachers and performers, beginners and lifelong singers, people shaped by different cultures, languages and professions. What we share is simple — a love of singing together, and the feeling of having finally found a place where every voice belongs.

Here, the free expression and creativity of each person is a right. We welcome diversity, we listen, and we make the practice of singing a tool for confidence, personal growth and genuine connection between people. Around these values, strangers become a community — and a community becomes a family.
