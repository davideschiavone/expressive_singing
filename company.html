Our Story

An Artistic Vision

Born from a profound belief in the voice as an instrument of transformation, Expressive Singing Company brings together artists who refuse to separate technique from soul. We create performances that speak to the human condition.

Our work spans opera, contemporary music, interdisciplinary performance and educational concerts. We are based in Geneva and perform across Switzerland and internationally.

Current production

Soundtrack

What if your life had a soundtrack?

Soundtrack is an intimate concert-performance in which four voices weave together songs, stories and silence to explore what music means to us — the songs that shaped us, comforted us, and set us free.

Duration: 90 min · All ages · French/English · Geneva premiere 2025

Artistic Director

Visionary vocal coach with 10 years of experience in expressive arts. Trained in classical and contemporary techniques.

Anna Moreau

Brings fierce energy and technical precision to every session. Anna specialises in contemporary styles and helping singers find their own, unmistakable sound.

Voice & Movement Teacher
